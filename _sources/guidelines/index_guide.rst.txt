Guidelines
##########

Good projects and their repos include clear guidelines and instructions for
user contribution. They help potential customers understand your project's
purpose. This section includes a variety of best-practice guidelines that
support collaboration, reuse, and accelerated development. Each guideline
lists an accompanied :ref:`template <index_tmpl>`.
